Projects

Fluffy Skirmish (2019)

Online Multiplayer 3rd person shooter developed in Unity3D.

We were really into the idea of a cute battle royale with a plot twist, so in this game you get to play as a bunnie with plastic pistols that shoot bubble gums. Our map mechanic works a bit differently: the players need to locate themselves by looking at the map and scouting their surroundings. If they fail to get out of the danger zone they'll fall into a pit of lava.

itch.io page

Supermarket Battle (2019)

Local multiplayer PvP developed in Unity3D.

We developed this game during BGS Game Jam (and won 2nd place!).

In this game you play against your friends and whoever gets the most items from the supermaket wins. There are no rules: you can crash the others' carts to drop their items or even steal their whole cart to yourself.
